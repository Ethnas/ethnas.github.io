Dragon harpoon

The dragon harpoon is a rare drop from wyrms. It requires 61 Fishing for catching tuna and swordfish, and it can catch sharks, at level 76. It gives the player a 20% faster catch rate compared to the regular Harpoon. Unlike the regular Harpoon variant, the dragon harpoon is wieldable, requiring 60 Attack to do so.

Special attack

The dragon harpoon has a special attack, Fishstabber, that provides a visible +3 boost to Fishing. This consumes 100% of the player's special attack energy, though it cannot be used without the 60 Attack required to wield the harpoon.
